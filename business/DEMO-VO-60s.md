# Pipeline — 60-second explainer (voiceover / About text)

_~140 words ≈ 55–60s at a natural pace. Use for a video VO, the landing page, or store copy._

---

Job hunting means juggling dozens of applications scattered across your inbox — and it's
almost impossible to keep track of who's replied, who's gone quiet, and what's next.

Pipeline fixes that. It reads your mailbox and turns every application email into a clean,
color-coded board, grouped by company — so you can see exactly where each one stands:
active, interview, offer, or rejected.

A recruiter called instead of emailing? Update the status in a single click. Applied
somewhere it didn't catch? Add it in five seconds.

And because Pipeline runs entirely on your own device — read-only, with nothing ever sent
to a server — your inbox stays completely private.

Connect Gmail, Outlook, or any email account, and in seconds your whole job search is
organized in one place.

Pipeline — your job hunt, straight from your inbox.
