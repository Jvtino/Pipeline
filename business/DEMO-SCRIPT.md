# Pipeline — 60-second demo script

_Internal. Local only. For B2B outreach + the landing page._

Two ways to make it (pick one):
- **A) Screen recording + voiceover** — most compelling. Record the app following the shot
  list below; add the voiceover (your voice, or paste the VO lines into ChatGPT's read-aloud).
- **B) Captioned screenshot montage** — no recording; stitch clean screenshots with the
  captions over music. Faster, less lively. (I can capture the screenshots for this.)

**Recording setup (for A):** size the Pipeline window to ~1280×800, demo data only (no real
inbox), hide your dock/menubar clutter, `Cmd+Shift+5` → record selected window. Move
deliberately and slowly — you'll trim later.

---

## The script (≈60s)

| Time | On screen (action) | Caption overlay | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:05 | App opens on the board | **Pipeline** | "Job hunting means dozens of applications buried in your inbox." |
| 0:05–0:12 | Slow pan over the company cards + stat tiles | Every application, one board | "Pipeline turns them into a clean, color-coded board — grouped by company." |
| 0:12–0:20 | Click a stat tile (e.g. Interview) to filter; click back to All | Active · Interview · Offer · Rejected | "See exactly where everything stands at a glance." |
| 0:20–0:32 | Click a status pill → menu → choose **Interview** | Update by hand in a click | "A recruiter called instead of emailing? Just set the status yourself — it's tracked instantly." |
| 0:32–0:44 | Click **+ Add position** → fill Company/Role → Add; new card appears | Nothing slips through | "Applied somewhere it didn't catch? Add it in five seconds." |
| 0:44–0:53 | Hover the "Demo data / synced" chip; show the Connect modal briefly | Runs on your device · read-only | "It reads your mailbox read-only and runs entirely on your device. Your email never leaves your machine." |
| 0:53–0:60 | Logo + tagline end card | **Pipeline — your job hunt, straight from your inbox** | "Pipeline. Your job hunt, straight from your inbox." |

---

## Shot list (exact clicks)
1. Open app (demo data showing).
2. Slow scroll down ~1 card, back up.
3. Click the **Interview** stat tile → pause → click **Total**.
4. Click any position's **status pill** → menu opens → click **Interview** → pill updates.
5. Click **+ Add position** → type "Northwind" / "Product Designer" → **Add position** → card appears.
6. Hover the top-left **sync chip**; click **Connect email** to flash the modal; close it.
7. Cut to the end card (logo + tagline).

## Where ChatGPT Plus helps
- **Voiceover:** paste the VO column into ChatGPT, ask for a read-aloud (or refine the tone, e.g. "warmer, 5% slower").
- **Intro/outro sting or music ideas** (Sora/Suno-style) — optional, keep it under 3s.
- **Variants:** ask GPT for a 15-second cut for ads and a 30-second cut for the landing page.

## What ChatGPT can NOT do
- Recreate the real Pipeline UI — that footage must be recorded from your screen.
