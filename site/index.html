Your job hunt, straight from your inbox.

Pipeline turns application emails into a clean, color‑coded dashboard — grouped by company, classified automatically, and kept entirely on your device.

Download for macOS & Windows
Works with Gmail, Outlook, and any IMAP mailbox · read‑only · runs locally

Color‑coded pipeline

Every application shows its stage at a glance: active, interview, offer, or rejected.

Grouped by company

All your roles at one company sit on a single card, sorted by latest activity.

On your device

No server. Your mail and credentials never leave your machine — read‑only, always.

Add what it misses

Recruiter called instead of emailing? Add a position by hand and set its status.

Privacy first. Pipeline reads your mailbox read‑only to find job‑application mail, and stores everything locally. It does not run a backend, show ads, or sell data. Read the full Privacy Policy and Terms.
